# Daisy 1.0.7.11 — transcription survives a mid-recording headphone switch

## Fixed

- Switching your audio device while recording — plugging in wired headphones, connecting Bluetooth, or moving to speakers — no longer breaks transcription. Before, if the new device ran at a different sample rate (common with wired headsets), the live transcript would freeze and that stretch went missing from the saved transcript too — even though the flower showed Daisy was still hearing you. Daisy now adapts to the new device on the fly and keeps transcribing without a gap.
