# Daisy 1.0.7.12 — your in-progress recording can't be cleaned up out from under you

## Fixed

- A long recording could lose its audio if something refreshed your library mid-session. A recording longer than five minutes has audio on disk but no transcript yet (the transcript is written when you press Stop), and Daisy's "tidy up leftover empty folders" cleanup could mistake the in-progress recording for leftover junk and remove it — most often when another app queried Daisy through its local server (for example an AI assistant connected to Daisy), or when the History list refreshed while you were still recording. Daisy now always protects the recording in progress, so it is never cleaned up until it has been safely saved.
