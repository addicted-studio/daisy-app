# Daisy 1.0.7.15 — clearer recording state, tidier settings

## New

- **You'll know when the other side isn't being recorded.** If Daisy can't capture a meeting's other side (Screen Recording permission missing, or audio playing to a device that can't be captured), the floating flower's center turns red and a banner appears in the window and the menu-bar panel — instead of quietly recording only your voice.
- **Transcription settings, simplified.** Model, dictation engine, language, and live-transcript detail now live in one block with plain names (Default / Highest Accuracy, Default / Faster) and a visible model-download progress.
- **Progress while summaries and follow-ups generate.** Re-summarizing or drafting a follow-up now shows an inline spinner, not just a quick toast.

## Fixed

- Library transcript no longer spills past the edge of its card, and speaker labels render in **bold** instead of showing raw `**` asterisks.
- The floating widget now matches Daisy's logo and gently "breathes" while it's working.
