# Daisy 1.0.7.16 — auto-record on time, smarter "other side" warning

## Changed

- **Simpler auto-start settings.** The Always / Selective / Prompt / Manual buttons are now a single "Auto-record" dropdown with plain-language choices — All meetings, Meetings with a link, Ask me first, Off — living in the Meetings settings group. The fiddly "Detection delay" knob is gone; Daisy just starts when a meeting app opens, and if it ever jumps the gun you can stop it from the "Recording started" notification.
- **Tidier Settings, regrouped.** Plain-language labels throughout ("Capture system audio" → "Record the other side", "Play sound…" → "Notification sounds", the floating recorder is just "Floating widget"), long sub-labels trimmed, and rows moved to where they belong — "Record the other side" sits with the Meetings options, "Notification sounds" with the other notifications, and the floating widget with Audio & devices. Shortcut rows read simply too ("Dictation," "Hold to talk, release to paste").
- **Auto-stop in one choice.** The "Stop when the meeting wraps up" toggle and the separate "Grace period" picker are now a single "Stop when the meeting ends" dropdown — Off, At the scheduled end, or 5 minutes after.
- **Periodic screenshots paused.** The "Capture screenshots periodically" option has been removed for now while we rework it.
- **Speaker settings, combined and in plain words.** "Diarization" and "Speaker matching" are now one "Speakers" block, and the jargon is gone — "Diarize microphone too" → "Split voices in my mic", "Suppress acoustic echo" → "Remove echo from my mic", "Match known speakers" → "Name known people".
- **Settings, reorganized for clarity.** A new **Recording** tab gathers your microphone, shortcuts, meeting auto-record, and on-screen widget in one place, so **General** is now just Profile, Storage, Privacy, and Notifications. Storage split into Storage / Privacy ("Delete audio after"); the "Recordings folder" is named for what it holds; model and engine choices read in plain words (Standard / Most accurate / Faster); and the model-cache row sits at the end of Transcription.
- **Notion lives with your other connections.** Sending finished recordings to Notion now sits on the **Connections** page next to your MCP integrations, instead of buried under Settings → Storage.
- **One word for a recording.** Daisy now consistently calls a saved meeting a "recording" (not "session"), and the auto-record prompt asks about a "meeting" rather than a "call."

## Fixed

- **Calendar auto-record now starts right on schedule.** Previously a meeting could begin recording up to ~30 seconds early and at an unpredictable moment (the calendar was only checked every 15 seconds). Daisy now arms a precise timer and starts recording exactly 5 seconds before the meeting's scheduled start — enough to catch the opening words, without the random pre-roll. If Daisy launches or wakes after a meeting has already begun, it still starts immediately (within the first 2 minutes).
- **The "couldn't reach the other side" warning now clears itself.** If the other side was simply quiet for the first couple of minutes, Daisy could show the "Only your voice" banner — and then keep showing it even after the other side started talking. The warning now disappears the moment real audio is captured from the other side (and the floating flower's center returns to normal).
- **Transcript view renders markdown properly.** Headings, the recording subtitle, and bullet/checklist lines now display as formatted text instead of showing raw `#`, `>`, and `-` characters (speaker labels were already bold).
