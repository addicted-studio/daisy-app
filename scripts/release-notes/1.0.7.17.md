# Daisy 1.0.7.17

- Long recordings no longer lose parts of the transcript. When you press Stop, Daisy now re-transcribes the complete recording from the saved audio on disk — so the final transcript and speaker labels cover the whole session, even long or fast-paced meetings where the live preview fell behind.
- The offline pass on Stop now spans the entire recording (not just the most recent stretch), so summaries and "who said what" are built from the full conversation.
