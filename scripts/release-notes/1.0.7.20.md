# Daisy 1.0.7.20

- Dictation vocabulary now does two jobs. On the Dictation page → Vocabulary → Add word, leave the toggle off to **teach Daisy a word** — a name, brand, or piece of jargon — so it spells and capitalises it the way you want; flip "Correct a misspelling" on to make a **replacement rule** (misheard → fix), the way the dictionary worked before. Each entry shows which kind it is, and you can edit or delete it inline.
- Words you teach don't just get fixed after the fact — they also nudge the transcription engine to actually hear them, so a name it used to mangle comes out right more often to begin with.
- Calmer colours. The bright amber accent that tinted selected filters, links and controls is now a light brown, and the Record button is a warm beige instead of green — less visual noise across the warm interface. The brown row-selection highlight is unchanged.
