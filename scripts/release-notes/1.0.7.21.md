# Daisy 1.0.7.21

- **Import from Apple Voice Memos.** Turn on Settings → Recording → "Import Voice Memos" and Daisy checks once a day, transcribes any new recordings on your Mac, and drops a plain-text note into a "Voice Memos" folder alongside your transcripts. No summaries, no cloud — the audio never leaves your Mac. iCloud recordings synced from your iPhone get picked up too.
- One-time setup: Daisy needs Full Disk Access to read your Voice Memos library, and points you straight to the right System Settings pane. Already have a back catalogue? Hit "Process existing recordings" to transcribe everything; otherwise Daisy only imports new memos from when you switch it on.
