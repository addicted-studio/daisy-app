# Daisy 1.0.7.24

- **Custom Library folders.** Settings → General → Folders now lets you create, rename and delete your own folders. Private, Work and Calls became editable too (Inbox and Notes stay fixed). New folders show up in the sidebar and the "Move to folder…" menu; deleting a folder moves its recordings to Inbox.
- **Pick a default folder for meetings.** Choose where calendar-bound and auto-started meetings get filed — it was always "Work". The folder you pick as the default can't be deleted until you switch the default away from it.
- **The Transcript panel shows just the conversation.** It no longer repeats the recording's title and "recorded …" line at the top (that's already in the header above), and "Copy Transcript" copies the clean transcript.
- **Tag from the toolbar.** A recording's tag now lives in a capsule next to Summarize — click it to type a new tag or pick one you've used before.
