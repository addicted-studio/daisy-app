# Daisy 1.0.7.26

- Daisy now recovers recordings interrupted by a crash, power loss, or force-quit. If your Mac dies mid-meeting, the recording is no longer lost — on the next launch Daisy rebuilds the transcript from the audio it had already saved and adds it to your Library.
- Recordings pause cleanly when your Mac goes to sleep and resume when it wakes; a graceful restart or shutdown saves the recording in progress.
- If you've set Storage to "Don't record audio", Daisy now warns that an interrupted recording can't be recovered in that mode — only sessions you finish keep their transcript.
