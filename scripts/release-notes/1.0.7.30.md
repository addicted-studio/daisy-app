# Daisy 1.0.7.30 — refreshed on-device speech models

## Changed

- Updated the on-device speech engine (FluidAudio 0.15.4). Parakeet transcription now uses the v3 model with broader language coverage (25 languages), and speaker diarization moves to the newer pyannote Community-1 model for cleaner speaker separation. Recording and transcription work exactly as before — the underlying models are just newer and more capable. The updated models download automatically the first time they're needed.
