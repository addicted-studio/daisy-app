# Daisy 1.0.7.36 — steadier briefs, clearer loading, tasks in their own list

## Fixed

- **The meeting prep brief no longer gets stuck loading.** If your summary provider wasn't ready or stopped responding, the "Assembling from your past meetings…" card could spin forever. It now checks the provider first and gives up gracefully after a timeout with a retry, instead of hanging.
- **Dictation with the Standard engine pastes your text again.** After switching the dictation engine to Standard, dictation could show that it was listening but then paste nothing — the transcription was being over-filtered and dropped before it reached the clipboard. Fixed; short and quiet phrases come through now.
- **The model-setup indicator actually looks like it's working.** During the one-time model load the progress bar could sit still and look frozen. It now animates, shows how much has downloaded (e.g. "412 / 626 MB"), and counts up the seconds while the model finishes loading — so you can see it's making progress, not stuck.

## Changed

- **Your open tasks now live in their own list.** Daisy used to tuck tasks under a meeting when it shared an attendee, but that sometimes filed a task under the wrong meeting with the same person. Tasks now sit together in their own "To close" / "Overdue" section instead.
