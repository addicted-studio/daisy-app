# Daisy 1.0.7.5 — recordings no longer lost on a mid-session mic change

## Fixed

- **Recordings could be discarded on Stop after a mic/route change — fixed (build 47).** When the input device changed during a session (Bluetooth connecting, or a device re-initialisation right after Start), Daisy rolls the audio into a new file part. The Stop-time "did we capture anything?" check only looked at the original file, so it could wrongly decide the session was empty and delete the whole thing — losing the recording **and** its transcript even though audio and a live transcript existed. Daisy now counts captured audio across all file parts, and never discards a session that produced a transcript (this also covers "Don't record audio" mode, where the transcript is the whole point). If you saw a recording vanish on Stop in 1.0.7.4, this is the fix.
- **Recording widget no longer flickers during "Preparing" (build 47).** The flower's centre stayed a calm, static white while the petals do the loading shimmer — the brief amber blink in the middle during start-up is gone.
