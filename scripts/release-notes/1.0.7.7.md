# Daisy 1.0.7.7 — calmer recording flower, reworked sounds, better echo cleanup

## Changed

- The recording flower is calmer and clearer: the loader no longer stair-steps on ProMotion displays, the centre stays steady (no flicker) while a summary is generating, and the "done" pop is gentler.
- Daisy now respects Reduce Motion (System Settings → Accessibility → Display): the spinning loader and the celebration bounce are replaced with a calm, static state.
- The three recording modes — meeting, dictation, voice note — now read at matched brightness, so no mode looks louder or dimmer than the others.

## Fixed

- Recording sounds reworked: a clear chime now plays the moment your transcript is ready (in sync with the flower), and a distinct, gentle cue plays if a recording fails. The pause / resume / stop clicks were removed — they fired mid-recording and could leak into meetings played through speakers.
- Better acoustic-echo cleanup: when you record a meeting through your speakers instead of headphones, far fewer duplicate lines slip into the transcript. Daisy now also catches echoes when one side captured a whole sentence and the other split it into pieces.
