# Daisy 1.0.7.9 — microphone reliability + a livelier recording flower

## Fixed

- Recording now reliably picks up your voice. Daisy captures the microphone at a lower level (directly through Core Audio), fixing cases where a recording came back empty or missed your side — most often the built-in mic or a wired headset on macOS 26, where the audio engine could lock onto the wrong sample rate and capture nothing.
- The recording flower animates again while you record. Under heavy live transcription the petals could freeze in place even though audio and the transcript were flowing — they now stay live to your voice.

## Changed

- Smoother capture under load. The microphone's disk writing and audio analysis moved off the real-time audio thread, so heavy live transcription no longer competes with capture — fewer audio glitches on busy machines.
